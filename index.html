A pragmatic engineer

A software engineer by day i run in a daily loop of code, eat, meet, sleep & repeat, but over the weekends i exit this loop to focus on my weekend projects like wood working, embedded programming, 3D Modelling/Printing and other skills. I love being lazy, just hanging around watching youtube tech videos (there is so much stuff to learn on web!), when i am not, i like to solve tiny real life day to day problems with help of technologies this is where my electronics hobbies take shape.

Open source being current love of the market i have delve into lot of languages Java, Java script, Node.js and have worked with lot of open source technologies Ardunio, Rasberry Pi, ESP32, ESP8266 on embeded side while Cloud Foundry on cloud vendors. On the other hand i have also experienced with closed source technologies like Microsoft.Net, C#, VB.Net out of all i feel C# and Node.js were the most impressive ones for me.

Finally i also spend time creating content for my Youtube Channel and publish my projects on Instructables and make occasional contributions on Github and phone developer on Windows Store
